ABOUT THE APP
ALLOWEE PLUS is an app where NYSC corps members serving in Nigeria can:
1. Obtain loans in less than 5 minutes on request with little interest.
2. Purchase items at a cheaper rate from passing-corps memberes in
the state/LGA they've been posted to.
3. Sell items to incoming/other corps members in the same state/LGA of service.
4. Get detailed descriptions to NYSC camps.
ALLOWEE PLUS connects to corps members and creates an environment for
thriving business transactions amongst corps members in the same region.
